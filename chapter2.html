About Chapter 2

Welcome to Chapter 2 of DS SYMPOSIA '25, featuring Aeronautical Engineering (AERO), Agricultural Engineering (AGRI), Civil Engineering (CIVIL), Food Technology (FOOD), and Mechanical Engineering (MECH) departments. This chapter brings together innovative minds, technical experts, and enthusiastic students to showcase their talents and exchange knowledge in these specialized domains.

Chapter 2 explores the fundamentals of engineering and technology in core industrial and agricultural sectors. This segment focuses on theoretical concepts and their practical implementations across aeronautical, agricultural, civil, food technology, and mechanical engineering fields.
