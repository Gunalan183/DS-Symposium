About Chapter 1

Welcome to Chapter 1 of DS SYMPOSIA '25, focusing on Biomedical Engineering (BME), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), and Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) departments. This chapter brings together innovative minds, technical experts, and enthusiastic students to showcase their talents and exchange knowledge in these specialized domains.

Chapter 1 explores the fundamentals of emerging technologies and their applications in solving real-world problems. This segment focuses on theoretical concepts and their practical implementations across biomedical, electronics, and electrical engineering fields.

Join us for an exciting journey of learning, innovation, and networking with industry professionals and academic experts. Participate in workshops, technical presentations, and competitions designed to enhance your skills and knowledge.
